Eco Charger Monitor

AI-Powered Monitoring for Your Solar and Wind Energy System

App Description

Eco Charger Monitor is an AI-powered app designed to track and display real-time data from your solar panel and wind turbine system. The app connects to an ESP8266 Wi-Fi module to provide live updates on energy generation, battery status, weather conditions, and system performance.

Key Features

  • Live Data Monitoring – Track wind speed, solar energy, battery voltage, and phone charging status.
  • Smart AI Alerts – Get notified when wind speeds are too low or when the system requires maintenance.
  • Retraction & Deployment Control – Remotely retract or deploy the wind turbine and solar panel based on real-time conditions.
  • Weather Insights – Monitor temperature and humidity to optimize renewable energy usage.
  • User-Friendly Dashboard – Simple and intuitive interface for real-time tracking.
  • AI-Powered Predictions – Smart recommendations on when to deploy wind or solar based on weather forecasts.
  • Voice Command Integration – Control the system using voice commands for hands-free operation.

How It Works

1. Connect to Wi-Fi: The app communicates with ESP8266 to fetch real-time data from your Arduino Mega-based Eco Charger system.

2. Monitor Performance: See wind turbine voltage, power generation, and battery charging status.

3. Receive AI Alerts: Get automatic recommendations when to retract the wind turbine and switch to solar energy.

4. Control System Remotely: Manually deploy or retract the turbine and solar panels using in-app buttons or voice commands.

Target Users

  • Renewable energy enthusiasts
  • Students & engineers working on smart energy projects
  • Off-grid home users wanting to track energy production
  • Smart home automation users
